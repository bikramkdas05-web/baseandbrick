Who We Are

A local firm built for the paperwork that protects real ground.

Base and Brick Engineering Consultant Pvt. Ltd. is an environmental and engineering consultancy headquartered in Rajbiraj, Saptari. We work with project proponents, contractors and public agencies to move development projects through Nepal's environmental clearance process — from initial screening to post-construction monitoring.

Our name reflects how we work: every assessment is built on a verified base — site visits, baseline data, stakeholder consultation — before a single brick of mitigation planning or regulatory documentation is laid on top. Reports assembled without that base don't survive review, or the monsoon.

We support hydropower developers, road and transmission line contractors, industries and municipal bodies across the Madhesh plains and the hill corridors that feed them.

Services

Compliance work, taken seriously at every stage.

From the first screening question to the last monitoring report, we handle the documentation and fieldwork that keeps a project inside the law.

01

Initial Environmental Examination (IEE)

Screening, baseline data collection and report preparation for projects that fall under Schedule 1 of Nepal's environmental regulations.

02

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

Full-scale impact assessment, scoping documents and terms of reference for larger infrastructure and hydropower projects.

03

Environmental Management Plans (EMP)

Site-specific mitigation, monitoring and institutional arrangements, built to be implementable by contractors and supervised by project owners.

04

Compliance Monitoring & Audit

Periodic monitoring visits, compliance status reports and third-party audits for projects already under implementation.

05

Social & Resettlement Support

Social impact screening, public consultation facilitation and support for land acquisition and compensation processes.

06

Regulatory Liaison

Coordination with the Ministry of Forests and Environment, provincial environment units and project-specific regulatory bodies through to clearance.

How We Work

A fixed sequence, because the regulation is a fixed sequence.

Environmental clearance in Nepal follows a defined order. We follow it too — nothing here is optional or reorderable.

01

Screening & Scoping

We classify the project against Nepal's environmental schedules, define the study boundary and draft the terms of reference.

02

Baseline & Field Investigation

Site visits, physical and biological baseline surveys, and consultation with affected communities and local government.

03

Impact Assessment

Identification and evaluation of predicted impacts across construction and operation phases.

04

EMP & Mitigation Design

Mitigation measures matched to each identified impact, with implementation responsibility and monitoring indicators assigned.

05

Regulatory Submission

Report finalization, public review coordination and submission to the relevant approving body.

06

Monitoring & Audit

Post-approval compliance monitoring through construction and operation, with periodic reporting.
